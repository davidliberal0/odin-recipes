Lasagna

Perfectly cooked lasagna that'll make your mouth water!

This recipe takes a couple of shortcuts by using store bought sauce and pre-packaged lasagna sheets. Using these pre-made ingredients will save you time without sacraficing flavor. Although we think it is perfect as is, we advise you to add your own little twitst to this recipe.

Ingredients

  • ¾ lb. lasagna noodles
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano
  • Kosher Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 (32-0z.) jars marinara
  • 16 oz. whole milk ricotta
  • ½ freshly grated Parmesan, divided
  • ¼ chopped parsley, plus more for garnish
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 lb. sliced mozzarella
