Blue Berry Muffins

Not too sweet and delightful most Blue Berry Muffins

These muffins are huge and tastey with a sugary crumb topping. I usually double the recipe to feed the entire family. I recommend to fill the muffin cups to the top edge for a much larger generously sized deli style muffin. You can even throw EXTRA blueberries in there as well!

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup milk, or more as needed
  • 1 cup of fresh blueberries
  • ⅓ cup all-pupose flour
  • ¼ cup butter, cubed
  • 1½ teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease muffin cups or line with muffin liners.
  2. Combine 1 1/2 cups flour, 3/4 cup sugar, salt and baking powder. Place vegetable oil into a 1 cup measuring cup; add the egg and add enough milk to reach the 1-cup mark.
  3. Mix this with flour mixture. Fold in blueberries.
  4. Fill muffin cups right to the top, and sprinkle with crumb topping mixture.
  5. To Make Crumb Topping: Mix together 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup flour, 1/4 cup butter, and 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon. Mix with fork, and sprinkle over muffins before baking.
  6. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, or until done.
