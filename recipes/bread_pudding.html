Bread Pudding

Beautiful baked bread pudding dish topped with powdered sugar

This recipe demonstrates that almost any left over bread can be easily made into a dessert without much effort

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, more for greasing pan
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ loaf sweet egg bread like challah or brioche, cut into 2-inch cubes (about 5 to 6 cups)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
